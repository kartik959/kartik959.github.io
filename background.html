HTML Background

The good background makes the web page look great!:)

<body> has two tags that configure the background.
The background can be color or image.

Bgcolor

<body bgcolor="#000000">
<body bgcolor="rgb(0,0,0)">
<body bgcolor="black">

Background

The background attribute sets the background to the image. The attribute value is the URL of the image.

<body background="clouds.jpg">

Example

<body bgcolor="#eeeeee">
